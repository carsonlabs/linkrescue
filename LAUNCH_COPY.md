# LinkRescue — Launch Copy

## Product Hunt

**Tagline (60 char):** Stop losing affiliate commissions to broken links

**Description:**
If you run affiliate content, your links are rotting right now. Programs change URL structures, merchants shut down, redirect chains break tracking parameters — and every broken link is a commission you'll never see.

LinkRescue crawls your sites daily, detects broken and redirected affiliate links, classifies the issue (404, redirect-to-homepage, lost tracking params), and shows you exactly which pages and links need fixing — with estimated revenue impact.

Connect your site in 30 seconds. Free tier monitors 1 site, 50 pages.

**First Comment (as maker):**
Hey PH! I built LinkRescue after realizing one of my own affiliate sites had 23 broken Amazon links — some had been dead for months. That's money I just left on the table.

The problem is that affiliate links break silently. The merchant changes their URL structure, your tag parameter gets stripped in a redirect, or the product page just disappears. You don't notice until you check your commission report and wonder why it dropped.

LinkRescue scans daily and catches: 404s, 5xx errors, timeouts, redirects to homepages (usually means the product was removed), and lost tracking parameters (your affiliate tag got stripped).

Free: 1 site, 50 pages, weekly scans. Pro ($29/mo): 5 sites, 500 pages, daily. Agency ($79/mo): 25 sites, unlimited pages, hourly + API.

Would love feedback from anyone running affiliate content.
